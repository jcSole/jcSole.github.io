  1. IJOC
    An Exact Method for (Constrained) Assortment Optimization Problems with Product Costs
    Markus Leitner, Andrea Lodi, Roberto Roberti, and 1 more author
    INFORMS Journal on Computing, 2024
    Abs arXiv Bib

    We study optimizing assortment decisions in the presence of product-specific costs when customers choose according to a multinomial logit model. We propose the first exact solution method for this NP-hard problem, finding provably optimal assortments for instances with up to 1,000 products in about 0.2 seconds. Our approach extends to handle cardinality constraints and mixed-multinomial logit models.

2022

  1. IJOC
    On the Estimation of Discrete Choice Models to Capture Irrational Customer Behaviors
    Sanjay Dominik Jena, Andrea Lodi, and Claudio Sole
    INFORMS Journal on Computing, 2022
    Abs arXiv Bib

    We propose an estimation method for the Generalized Stochastic Preference choice model, which subsumes the family of Random Utility Maximization models and is capable of capturing halo effects. We show how to use partially-ranked preferences to efficiently model rational and irrational customer types from transaction data. Our approach employs column generation with a novel dominance rule to extract customer types. Testing on grocery and drug store data shows that accounting for irrational preferences can boost predictive accuracy by 12.5% on average.

  1. IJOO
    A Partially Ranked Choice Model for Large-Scale Data-Driven Assortment Optimization
    Sanjay Dominik Jena, Andrea Lodi, Hugo Palmer, and 1 more author
    INFORMS Journal on Optimization, 2020
    Abs Bib

    We propose an embedded column-generation procedure to efficiently estimate partially ranked choice models from historical transaction and assortment data. We use a growing preference tree that represents partially ranked preferences to learn preferences and optimize assortments for thousands of products.
